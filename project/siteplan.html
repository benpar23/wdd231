WDD 231 Final Project Site Plan

Site Name

The name of this website will be Brownies Squared, a play on words for the brownie squares being baked and sold in this business.

Site Purpose

This website will be for a brownie business titled Brownies Squared where customers and interested parties can come to learn more about the business, make an order, and even find recipes to try themselves.

Scenarios

What kind of brownies does Brownies Squared sell?

Who is the person/owner behind the brownies?

Where is the business located?

I want to buy brownies!

Color Schema

The main background color used in the header as well as in certain sections will be rgb(228,228,200).

The secondary background color used in the footer as well as perhaps at the bottom of certain sections will be rgb(55,5,5)

Typography

The font being used for the website will be "Outfit". No other fonts will be used.
